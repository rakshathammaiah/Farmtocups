Our Artisanal Process

A Taste of Coorg's Legacy

Every sip of FarmtoCups transports you to the misty hills of Coorg, where generations of skilled artisans have perfected their craft.

The rich, robust flavors embody centuries of tradition, sustainable practices, and the unique terroir of the Western Ghats. More than just a beverage, it's a direct connection to the farmers, their heritage, and the lush landscapes that make Coorg's produce truly exceptional.

Hand-Picking the Finest Beans

We start by sourcing only the highest quality Robusta cherries from our partner farms. Each cherry is handpicked at the peak of ripeness, ensuring the richest flavor.

Traditional
Processing

The cherries are carefully processed using the washed method, where the pulp is removed, and the beans are fermented and dried under the sun. This method enhances the coffee’s smoothness and flavor complexity.

Specialised
Roasting

After roasting, the beans are expertly blended and ground to perfection. Our packaging is designed to keep the coffee fresh and aromatic, with each bag sealed to maintain the beans’ rich flavors.

Our Story

FarmtoCups was born in the misty hills of Coorg, where coffee isn't just a crop—it's a way of life.

FarmtoCups was born in the misty hills of Coorg, where coffee isn't just a crop—it's a way of life. Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring the rich, bold flavors of Coorg's finest Robusta beans directly to coffee lovers worldwide, while honoring the farmers who make it all possible.

Our commitment goes beyond just great coffee. We believe in:
1. Sustainable Practices: Working in harmony with nature, just as Coorg's farmers have done for centuries.
2. Fair Partnerships: Ensuring our farmers receive fair compensation for their artisanal craft.
3. Preserving Heritage: Keeping alive the time-honored techniques that make Coorg's coffee unique.

Every cup of FarmtoCups coffee tells a story—of misty mornings in the Western Ghats, of carefully hand-picked cherries, of artisanal roasting, and of the passionate farmers who pour their hearts into every bean

The Farmers’ Legacy

Crafting Coffee with Passion

For generations, the farmers of Coorg have cultivated coffee with unparalleled dedication and expertise. We partnered with these custodians of tradition, like fourth-generation farmer Ravi Kumar and the women's cooperative led by Lakshmi Aunty, to preserve and share their legacy.

One of our partners, Ravi Kumar, is a fourth-generation coffee farmer. His family has been cultivating Robusta coffee on their 5-acre estate in the lush coffee belt of Coorg. Ravi’s grandfather planted the first coffee trees in the 1940s, and since then, the family has continued to perfect their craft. Ravi’s daily routine includes handpicking the ripest cherries, a process he learned from his ancestors. This labor-intensive method ensures that only the best beans are selected for roasting.

Another key partner, Lakshmi Aunty, manages a cooperative of local women farmers. This cooperative was formed to empower women in the community and promote fair trade practices. Lakshmi Aunty and her team handpick coffee cherries and process them with a meticulous approach passed down through generations. Their commitment to quality and sustainability ensures that every bean embodies the essence of Coorg’s coffee heritage.
