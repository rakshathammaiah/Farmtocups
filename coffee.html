Robusta Coffee

Robusta Coffee - Parchment AAA

Experience the bold essence of Coorg with our premium Robusta Coffee - Parchment AAA. This exceptional brew embodies the rich heritage and unique terroir of the Western Ghats.

Handpicked from the misty hills of Coorg at altitudes of 1,000-1,200 meters, our Parchment AAA Robusta beans are nurtured by generations of expertise and the perfect microclimate.

Carefully processed using the traditional parchment method, which enhances the coffee's natural flavors and results in a cleaner, more refined cup.

Sourced directly from our partner farms, ensuring fair compensation for our farmers and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

Perfect for South Indian filter coffee. Use with a traditional filter coffee maker for an authentic experience. Pairs wonderfully with milk for a rich, creamy brew.

Packed in triple-layer bags with a one-way degassing valve. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Best consumed within 3 months of roasting date for optimal flavor profile.

Roast Level: Medium (Agtron scale: 55-60)
Roasting Temperature: Initial 185°C, dropping to 175°C, with a total roast time of 12-14 minutes
First Crack: Occurs around 195°C
Development Time: 15-18% of total roast time

Grind Size: Medium (500-700 microns)
Particle Distribution: Uniform, with minimal fines
Ideal for: South Indian filter coffee, French press, and cold brew methods

Fragrance/Aroma: Intense, with notes of dark chocolate, toasted nuts, and a hint of spice
Flavor: Bold and full-bodied with a deep, powerful taste profile
Acidity: Low to medium (pH 5.2-5.5)
Body: Full, creamy mouthfeel
Sweetness: Subtle, with caramel undertones
Aftertaste: Long-lasting, with a pleasant bitterness and nutty finish

The Tale of Coorg's Finest: Robusta Coffee - Parchment AAA.

In the misty hills of Coorg, where the morning fog kisses the coffee plants, a story of passion unfolds with every bean. Our Robusta Coffee - Parchment AAA is not just a beverage; it's a legacy steeped in generations of expertise.

Picture Ravi, a fourth-generation coffee farmer, stepping out at dawn. The cool mountain air fills his lungs as he surveys the lush plantation his great-grandfather started in the 1940s. With practiced eyes, he selects only the ripest cherries, each one a promise of the rich brew to come.

These carefully chosen beans embark on a journey of transformation. Under the watchful eye of Lakshmi Aunty, leader of the local women's cooperative, they undergo the traditional parchment process. It's a labor of love, where each step - from washing to sun-drying - is performed with meticulous care, just as it has been for centuries.

The result? A medium-roast, medium-grind coffee that tells the story of its origin in every sip. As you brew it in your filter coffee maker, the aroma transports you to the verdant slopes of the Western Ghats. The deep, powerful flavor unfolds on your palate - a symphony of dark chocolate notes and earthy undertones, culminating in a smooth, full-bodied taste that lingers like a fond memory.

This is more than just your morning cup. It's a connection to the land, to the farmers, to a tradition of excellence. It's the story of Coorg, distilled into a rich, invigorating brew

Perfect for those who appreciate coffee with character, our Parchment AAA Robusta stands as a testament to the art of coffee cultivation. Whether enjoyed black to savor its complex notes, or with milk for a creamy indulgence, each cup is an homage to the misty mountains and the hands that nurture this exceptional coffee.

Embrace the tale. Savor the tradition. Experience the essence of Coorg with every sip of our Robusta Coffee - Parchment AAA.
