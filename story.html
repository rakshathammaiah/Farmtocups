FarmtoCups is a pioneering beverage company rooted in the rich agricultural heritage of Coorg, India. Our mission is to bring the authentic flavors of this region directly to consumers worldwide, while championing sustainable farming practices and supporting local communities.

Born from a vision to showcase Coorg's exceptional produce, FarmtoCups began as a passion project to connect urban consumers with the pure, unadulterated tastes of rural India. Our journey started with the iconic Robusta coffee beans of Coorg, but quickly expanded to embrace the full spectrum of beverages that this bountiful region has to offer.

While our signature Robusta coffee remains a cornerstone of our brand, FarmtoCups has grown to include a diverse range of beverages, each telling its own story of Coorg's terroir:
1. Artisanal Coffee: Bold, full-bodied Robusta with notes of dark chocolate
2. Exotic Teas: Handpicked and carefully processed local tea varieties
3. Fruit Juices: Made from Coorg's succulent seasonal fruits
4. Herbal Infusions: Crafted from indigenous herbs and spices
5. Specialty Drinks: Innovative blends that showcase Coorg's unique flavors

Our Farmers

The heart of FarmtoCups beats in the hands of our dedicated farmers. From Ravi Kumar's fourth-generation coffee estate to Lakshmi Aunty's women's cooperative, our network of small-scale farmers embodies generations of agricultural wisdom. We work closely with these artisans, ensuring fair compensation and promoting sustainable farming methods that preserve Coorg's biodiversity.
